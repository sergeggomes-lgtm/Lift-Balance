Chest
Chest area	Best exercises
Upper chest / clavicular pec	Incline dumbbell press, low-to-high cable fly
Mid chest	Flat dumbbell press, machine chest press
Lower chest	Weighted dips, decline press
Inner chest emphasis	Cable fly/crossover, pec deck
Full chest compound	Barbell bench press, push-ups weighted if needed
Back
Back area	Best exercises
Lats, width	Pull-ups/chin-ups, lat pulldown
Lower lats	One-arm cable pulldown, neutral-grip pulldown
Mid-back / rhomboids	Chest-supported row, seated cable row
Upper back / traps	Barbell row, face pulls
Rear delts/upper back	Reverse pec deck, rear-delt cable fly
Spinal erectors/lower back	Romanian deadlift, back extension
Full back compound	Deadlift, pull-up, row variation
Shoulders
Shoulder area	Best exercises
Front delts	Overhead press, incline press
Side delts	Cable lateral raise, dumbbell lateral raise
Rear delts	Reverse pec deck, rear-delt cable fly
Full shoulder compound	Seated dumbbell press, standing barbell press
Shoulder health/stability	Face pulls, cable external rotations
Legs
Leg area	Best exercises
Quads	Squat, leg press
Rectus femoris/quads	Leg extension, front squat
Hamstrings, hip-hinge	Romanian deadlift, good morning
Hamstrings, knee-flexion	Seated leg curl, lying leg curl
Glutes	Hip thrust, Bulgarian split squat
Glute medius/outer hip	Cable hip abduction, lateral band walks
Adductors/inner thigh	Adductor machine, sumo squat
Calves, gastrocnemius	Standing calf raise, donkey calf raise
Calves, soleus	Seated calf raise, bent-knee calf raise
Full lower body compound	Squat, trap-bar deadlift, walking lunge
Biceps
Biceps area	Best exercises
Long head / outer biceps	Incline dumbbell curl, Bayesian cable curl
Short head / inner biceps	Preacher curl, spider curl
Brachialis	Hammer curl, cross-body hammer curl
Forearm/brachioradialis	Reverse curl, Zottman curl
Full biceps	Standing barbell curl, cable curl
Triceps

Medial head	Reverse-grip pressdown, dips
